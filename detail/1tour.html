Magical Kerala Holiday

Kerala 7-Day, 6-Night Tour Plan

Kerala, known as "God's Own Country," offers a perfect blend of lush green hill stations, serene backwaters, and pristine beaches. This 7-day, 6-night tour covers Munnar, Thekkady, Alleppey, Kovalam, and Trivandrum, ensuring an unforgettable experience.

Day 1: Arrival in Kochi – Transfer to Munnar

Arrive at Cochin International Airport/Railway Station and proceed to Munnar, a picturesque hill station. En route, visit Cheeyappara & Valara Waterfalls and scenic tea plantations. Upon arrival, check in at the hotel and relax for the day. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 2: Munnar Sightseeing

Explore Munnar’s beauty by visiting Eravikulam National Park, home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr. Visit the Tea Museum and explore Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, Kundala Lake, Top Station, and Blossom Park. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 3: Munnar to Thekkady

Travel to Thekkady, known for the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. Enjoy a boat ride at Periyar Lake, spot wildlife, and visit a spice plantation. Optional activities include an elephant ride and watching Kathakali and Kalaripayattu performances. Overnight stay in Thekkady.

Day 4: Thekkady to Alleppey – Houseboat Stay

Proceed to Alleppey, famous for its backwaters. Check into a luxury houseboat and enjoy a scenic cruise through palm-fringed canals, witnessing Kerala’s village life. Relish delicious Kerala-style meals onboard. Overnight stay in the houseboat.

Day 5: Alleppey to Kovalam

Travel to Kovalam, a charming beach town. Check in at the hotel and relax at Kovalam Beach, Lighthouse Beach, and Hawa Beach. Enjoy stunning sunset views. Overnight stay in Kovalam.

Day 6: Kovalam & Trivandrum Sightseeing

Visit Trivandrum attractions, including the famous Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Napier Museum, Kuthiramalika Palace, and Shanghumugham Beach. Return to Kovalam for a relaxing evening. Overnight stay in Kovalam.

Day 7: Departure from Trivandrum/Kochi

Enjoy a leisurely morning before departing for Trivandrum Airport or Kochi Airport, concluding this mesmerizing Kerala tour.

Tour Inclusions

This package includes accommodation in 3-star/4-star hotels and a houseboat, daily breakfast, private AC cab transfers, and sightseeing as per the itinerary. Optional activities such as cultural performances and adventure experiences can be added based on preference.
