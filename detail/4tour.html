5-Day Rajasthan Tour Plan by Bus

Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur

Morning: Depart by bus and arrive in Jaipur.

Afternoon: Check into the hotel and visit City Palace, Jantar Mantar.

Evening: Explore local markets and visit Hawa Mahal.

Overnight: Stay in Jaipur.

Day 2: Jaipur Sightseeing

Morning: Visit Amber Fort and Nahargarh Fort.

Afternoon: Visit Jaigarh Fort and Albert Hall Museum.

Evening: Leisure time or optional cultural show.

Overnight: Stay in Jaipur.

Day 3: Jaipur to Jodhpur

Morning: Depart for Jodhpur (5-hour bus journey).

Afternoon: Visit Mehrangarh Fort and Jaswant Thada.

Evening: Explore Umaid Bhawan Palace and local markets.

Overnight: Stay in Jodhpur.

Day 4: Jodhpur to Udaipur

Morning: Depart for Udaipur (6-hour bus journey).

Afternoon: Visit City Palace and Jagdish Temple.

Evening: Boat ride on Lake Pichola.

Overnight: Stay in Udaipur.

Day 5: Udaipur Sightseeing & Departure

Morning: Visit Saheliyon Ki Bari and Fateh Sagar Lake.

Afternoon: Visit Sajjangarh Palace and depart for home.
