Char Dham

Char Dham Yatra from Ahmedabad

13 Days / 12 Nights

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Depart from Ahmedabad to Delhi by train. Overnight journey.
  • Day 2: Arrive in Delhi. Transfer to Haridwar by bus. Evening Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri. Overnight in Haridwar.
  • Day 3: Haridwar to Barkot via Mussoorie. Visit Kempty Falls en route. Overnight in Barkot.
  • Day 4: Barkot to Yamunotri and return. Trek or hire a pony/palki. Overnight in Barkot.
  • Day 5: Barkot to Uttarkashi. Visit Vishwanath Temple. Overnight in Uttarkashi.
  • Day 6: Uttarkashi to Gangotri and return. Participate in rituals. Overnight in Uttarkashi.
  • Day 7: Uttarkashi to Guptkashi. Scenic drive along riverbanks. Overnight in Guptkashi.
  • Day 8: Guptkashi to Kedarnath. Drive to Gaurikund, then trek or take a helicopter to Kedarnath. Overnight in Kedarnath.
  • Day 9: Kedarnath to Guptkashi. Return trek and drive. Overnight in Guptkashi.
  • Day 10: Guptkashi to Badrinath via Joshimath. Visit Narsingh Temple in Joshimath. Overnight in Badrinath.
  • Day 11: Badrinath to Rudraprayag. Morning darshan at Badrinath Temple. Visit Mana Village. Overnight in Rudraprayag.
  • Day 12: Rudraprayag to Haridwar via Rishikesh. Visit Laxman Jhula and Ram Jhula. Overnight in Haridwar.
  • Day 13: Haridwar to Delhi by bus. Board train to Ahmedabad. Overnight journey.
