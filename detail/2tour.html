Peaceful Kashmir Holiday

Kashmir 6-Day, 5-Night Tour Plan

Explore the beauty of Kashmir with this 6-day itinerary covering Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, and Sonmarg. Enjoy breathtaking landscapes, adventure activities, and serene lakes.

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar – Local Sightseeing

Arrive at Srinagar Airport and transfer to the hotel/houseboat. Enjoy a Shikara ride on Dal Lake, visit the Mughal Gardens (Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, Chashme Shahi), and explore Shankaracharya Temple. Stroll around Lal Chowk Market for shopping. Overnight stay in Srinagar.

Day 2: Srinagar to Gulmarg (50 km, 2 hours)

Drive to Gulmarg, known as the "Meadow of Flowers". Enjoy the Gondola Ride (Asia’s highest cable car) to Apharwat Peak. Visit Maharani Temple, St. Mary’s Church, and Strawberry Valley. Experience snow activities (in winter) like skiing, snowboarding, and sledging. Return to Srinagar in the evening. Overnight stay in Srinagar.

Day 3: Srinagar to Pahalgam (90 km, 3 hours) – Valley Tour

Drive to Pahalgam, the "Valley of Shepherds". En route, visit Awantipora Ruins and Saffron Fields. Explore Aru Valley, Betaab Valley, and Chandanwari (by local cab). Enjoy river rafting on the Lidder River (optional). Overnight stay in Pahalgam.

Day 4: Pahalgam to Srinagar – Leisure Day

Enjoy a relaxed morning in Pahalgam. Drive back to Srinagar and check in at the hotel. Free time for leisure or shopping at Lal Chowk Market. Overnight stay in Srinagar.

Day 5: Srinagar to Sonmarg (80 km, 2.5 hours) – Day Trip

Drive to Sonmarg, the "Meadow of Gold". Visit Thajiwas Glacier (horse ride optional). Explore Zoji La Pass and Baltal Valley (optional). Enjoy local food and breathtaking mountain views. Return to Srinagar in the evening. Overnight stay in Srinagar.

Day 6: Departure from Srinagar

After breakfast, transfer to Srinagar Airport for departure. End of a beautiful Kashmir tour.
