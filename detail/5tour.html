Uttarakhand Retreat

Uttarakhand Tour Plan - 6 Days

Day 1: Ahmedabad to Dehradun

Flight from Ahmedabad to Dehradun. Transfer to Mussoorie. Check-in at hotel and visit Mall Road & Kempty Falls.

Flight Details: Direct flights available from Ahmedabad to Dehradun. Average duration: 2 hours.

Day 2: Mussoorie Sightseeing

Visit Gun Hill, Camel’s Back Road, Company Garden, and Lal Tibba.

Day 3: Mussoorie to Rishikesh

Drive to Rishikesh. Visit Ram Jhula, Laxman Jhula, Triveni Ghat, and attend Ganga Aarti.

Day 4: Rishikesh to Nainital

Drive to Nainital. Check-in and visit Naini Lake, Naina Devi Temple, and The Mall Road.

Day 5: Nainital Sightseeing

Explore Snow View Point, Tiffin Top, Bhimtal, Sattal, and Naukuchiatal.

Day 6: Nainital to Dehradun - Ahmedabad

Drive back to Dehradun and take a flight to Ahmedabad.

Flight Details: Direct flights available from Dehradun to Ahmedabad. Average duration: 2 hours.
