Best of Varanasi, Prayagraj & Ayodhya

4-Day, 3-Night Tour Plan: Varanasi, Prayagraj & Ayodhya


Day 1: Arrival in Varanasi

Morning: Flight from Ahmedabad to Varanasi.

Afternoon: Check into the hotel.

Evening: Witness the Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat.

Overnight: Stay in Varanasi.

Day 2: Varanasi Sightseeing

Early Morning: Boat ride on the Ganges at sunrise.

Morning to Afternoon: Visit Kashi Vishwanath, Sankat Mochan, and Durga Temple.

Evening: Explore Sarnath.

Overnight: Stay in Varanasi.

Day 3: Varanasi to Ayodhya via Prayagraj

Morning: Depart for Prayagraj (3-hour drive).

Midday: Visit Triveni Sangam.

Afternoon: Travel to Ayodhya (4-hour drive).

Evening: Visit Ram Janmabhoomi and Hanuman Garhi.

Overnight: Stay in Ayodhya.

Day 4: Ayodhya to Varanasi

Morning: Visit Kanak Bhawan and Nageshwarnath Temple.

Afternoon: Return to Varanasi (4-hour drive) for departure.
