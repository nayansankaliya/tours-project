Himachal 6-Day, 5-Night Honeymoon Tour Plan

Celebrate your love with a romantic honeymoon in the scenic beauty of Himachal Pradesh. This 6-day, 5-night tour covers Shimla, Manali, and Solang Valley, offering the perfect mix of adventure, romance, and relaxation.

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi – Transfer to Shimla

Arrive at Delhi Airport and board a Volvo bus or private cab to Shimla (approx. 8 hours). Check-in at a romantic honeymoon suite and enjoy a special candlelight dinner. Overnight stay in Shimla.

Day 2: Shimla & Kufri Sightseeing

Visit the beautiful hill town of Kufri for horse riding and snow activities (seasonal). Explore The Ridge, Mall Road, Jakhoo Temple, and Christ Church. Enjoy an evening walk on the Mall Road with your partner. Overnight stay in Shimla.

Day 3: Shimla to Manali (250 km, 7 hours)

After breakfast, depart for Manali, passing through scenic valleys. En route, visit Pandoh Dam and Kullu Valley. On arrival, check in at a honeymoon-themed hotel. Enjoy a romantic flower bed decoration. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 4: Solang Valley Excursion

Visit Solang Valley for adventure activities like paragliding, skiing, and snow activities (seasonal). Spend quality time together in the beautiful surroundings. Return to Manali and enjoy a special honeymoon cake. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 5: Manali Local Sightseeing

Explore Hadimba Temple, Vashisht Hot Springs, Manu Temple, and Mall Road. Enjoy a romantic riverside picnic and shop for souvenirs. Relax in a cozy café with your partner. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 6: Departure from Manali to Delhi

After breakfast, check out and drive back to Delhi Airport for your return flight, ending your memorable honeymoon trip.
